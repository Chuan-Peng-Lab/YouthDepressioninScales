2_3_0_Reference Material
	包含了Eiko Fried的结果，作为参考
        Contains the results from Eiko Fried, used as a reference dataset.
2_3_1_First Trial
	第一次尝试，进行了包含五个量表 
	MFQ-C、cdi、sds、ces-d儿童版、汉密尔顿抑郁量表（HAMD）
	的内容分析——将量表间相同症状的题目归为一行
        The first trial, including five scales — MFQ-C, CDI, SDS, CES-D (Child Version), and HAMD — 
        conducted a content analysis in which items referring to the same symptom across scales were grouped into a single row.
2_3_2_Second Trial
	第二次尝试，进行了包含六个量表 
	dsi、bdi-II、ces-d、ces-d简版、中学生抑郁量表（cssds）、dsrsc
	的内容分析——将量表间相同症状的题目归为一行
        The second trial, involving six scales — DSI, BDI-II, CES-D, CES-D Short Form, CSSDS, and DSRSC —
        performed a content analysis aligning items measuring identical symptoms across scales into the same row.
2_3_3_Third Trial
	包含了对于first trial和second trial的11个量表的校验。
	并且重新对症状的顺序进行了排序，将相似的症状放在的临近的行
        Contains a verification of the 11 scales included in both the First and Second Trials, 
        with a reordering of symptom rows to place conceptually similar symptoms adjacent to each other.
2_3_4_Fourth Trial

	CBCL-1991、CBCL-2001、SMFQ、SCL-90、Andrensen（1994）简式抑郁量表、PHQ-9、
	医院焦虑与抑郁量表HAHD、Kutcher青少年抑郁量表﻿（KADS-11）、DASS-21、中学生心理健康量表（MSSMHS）王极盛、
	简明症状评定量表（BSRS-5）、抑郁自编量表 sakuma2010、中国教育小组调查量表（CEPS）、﻿
	中国青少年健康相关 危险行为调查问卷(初中)、抑郁自编题目Jiayi Gu 2020
	的内容分析——将量表间相同症状的题目归为一行。
        Includes the content analysis for all remaining scales not covered in the previous trials.
