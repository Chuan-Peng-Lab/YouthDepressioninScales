2_1_Inner Scales_Items Coding
	量表内部，将每个题目编码为症状
        Within each scale, every individual item is coded into corresponding symptoms.
2_2_Inner Scales_Items Combining
	量表内部，将测量同一个症状的题目合并，并重新赋予症状名
        Within each scale, items measuring the same symptom are merged and assigned a unified         
        symptom name.
2_3_Inter Scales_Items Combining
	量表之间，将测量同一种症状的题目汇总在一行，从而得到所有抑郁涉及的症状
        Across different scales, items measuring the same symptom are consolidated into one row,           
        producing a complete list of depression-related symptoms.
2_4_Inter Scales_Syptoms Combining
	尽管已经将测量同一种症状的题目放在了一行。但是症状的数目太多。因此，我们将类似的症状进行合并。
        Although items measuring the same symptom have been combined, the total number of symptoms         
        remains large; thus, similar symptoms are further merged for simplification.
2_5_Inter Scales_Syptoms Coding
	对每个量表下的题目所属的症状进行编码，哪些属于复合症状，哪些属于特殊症状。
        For each scale, symptoms are coded to distinguish compound versus specific symptoms.
2_6_Inner Scales_Items Combining_DC
	重新对2_2_Inner Scales_Items Combining进行double check，检查之前所做有没有不合理的地方。
         A double-check of 2_2_Inner Scales_Items Combining results to verify the consistency and          
        correctness of previous item merging.

        使用Literature_Search的结果进行内容分析，即将能够归类到同一个内容的题项放在同一行。
        (由于先前对题目的收集属于探索阶段，此处指的是形成规范化题目收集流程后，有一些问卷进行了更新并且本次采用的是完整流程，
       也是正文中提到的内部一致性的数据来源。具体数据来源为4members_output.xlsx，此为两组之间一致性数据来源。haoyuan_output.xlsx,liuqing_output.xlsx         
       这俩为组A两成员之间数据来源。Weibiao_output.xlsx此为组B两成员之间差异的数据来源，具体为标黄的地方是weibiao和mengzhen有差异的地方）
         Based on Literature_Search findings, perform content analysis by grouping items sharing the same conceptual meaning into the same row.

2_8_Scale Type & Symptom Clusters
        判断问卷是诊断类还是筛查类，以及判断抑郁症状可以分为几个症状群。
        Identify whether each questionnaire is diagnostic or screening, and categorize depression      
        symptoms into distinct symptom clusters.
